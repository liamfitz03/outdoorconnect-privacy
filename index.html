OutdoorConnect Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address and name when you create an account. On Android devices, with your permission, we access app usage statistics to track social media screen time. This data is processed on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide the OutdoorConnect service, calculate rewards points, and show you relevant local businesses and activities.

3. Data Storage

Account data is stored securely using Supabase. Screen time data is stored locally on your device only and is never shared with third parties.

4. Third Party Services

We use Google Maps to display activity locations. We use Firebase for push notifications. These services have their own privacy policies.
